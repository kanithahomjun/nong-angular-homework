SwiftView quickly and easily views and prints PCL, TIFF, HPGL and other files. Options include color viewing and PDF input.
SwiftConvert batch converts multiple full-color files to PCL, HPGL, PNG, PostScript, TIFF and PDF. Convert from PDF files with the PDF upgrade.
